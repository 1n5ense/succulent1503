Succulent plants are trendy for a reason. With juicy leaves, stems, or roots, succulents form a vast and diverse group of plants, offering easy-care choices for your home.

The color variation of succulents seems almost endless: blue-green, chartreuse, pink, red, yellow, white, burgundy, almost black, variegated, and more. The leaves may be rounded, needlelike, berrylike, ruffled, or spiky.
Many have an enticing "touch-me" quality.

Outdoor
Succulent
Garden

Succulents, with their striking forms, unusual colors, and easy-care reputation, are becoming increasingly important design elements in many gardens.
They can be planted in mass, as specimens in the garden, or even featured in containers.

Succulent garden design is appropriate for warm, temperate and even cold season locations. Succulent plants thrive in sunny, well drained areas with very little maintenance. Although they are very tolerant of dry conditions, succulent plants do need water, especially during the growing season.
